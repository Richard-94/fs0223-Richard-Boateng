Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. it will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image:Sally Vegas

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teasdpoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukerwarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the plan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a aprking knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though l sometimes knead it a afew times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
